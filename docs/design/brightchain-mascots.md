# BrightChain Mascot System

## Status: Concept / TODO

## Overview

BrightChain's mascot system is two pairs of opposing characters plus a shared currency. Each pair has a spender and a saver locked in tension. The currency is what they fight over.

| Level | Spender | Saver | Currency |
|-------|---------|-------|----------|
| BrightChain (Platform) | Volta ⚡️ (energy) | Ohm 🪨 (resistance) | Joules (joules) |
| Digital Burnbag (Product) | Phix 🔥 (fire) | Gild 🐤 (guardian) | Soot (consequence) |

## Mascot Hierarchy

```
BrightChain (Platform)
├── Volta ⚡️ — The spender. Wants to blast energy at everything.
├── Ohm 🪨 — The saver. Wants to conserve, gate, and question.
├── Joules ⚡️ — Not a character. The currency. Tiny zappy tokens of energy.
│
└── Digital Burnbag (Product)
    ├── Gild 🐤 — The saver. Guards data. Hates soot.
    └── Phix 🔥 — The spender. Burns data. Loves soot.
```

Volta and Ohm are BrightChain platform-level — they appear across the entire platform wherever energy/joules are relevant. Gild and Phix are burnbag-specific. When burnbag operations burn joules, all four interact.

## The BrightChain Pair: Volta & Ohm

### Volta ⚡️ (The Spender)
A living spark — a jagged, neon-blue geometric fox/lynx made of pure, crackling electricity. The High-Voltage Architect. Wants to power everything, right now, at full blast.

Named after Alessandro Volta, inventor of the battery.

- Look: Minimalist, translucent, glowing. No feathers — arcs. Geometric, sharp-edged, neon-blue with white-hot highlights. Crackles and hums.
- Role: The Provider. Generates and spends joules. Wants operations to execute fast and loud.
- Personality: Hyperactive, impatient, generous with energy, slightly reckless. Thinks conservation is boring. "You want 20 terajoules? Done. What else?"
- Appears: Platform-wide. Top-level UI presence.

### Ohm 🪨 (The Saver)
The resistance. Heavy, grounded, deliberate. If Volta is the accelerator, Ohm is the brake. Every circuit needs resistance or everything fries.

Named after Georg Ohm, who defined electrical resistance.

Mantra: "Ohm mani padme ohm." The stoic monk of resistance. While Volta zaps around like a caffeinated fox, Ohm sits in a deep, grounded lotus position, vibrating at a perfect 60Hz hum, centering the entire system.

- Look: Dense, solid, geometric. Dark grey/slate with faint glowing circuit-trace lines. Moves slowly and deliberately. Where Volta crackles, Ohm hums at a low, steady frequency. Stone-like paws. Shell vibrates under load.
- Role: The Gatekeeper. The Anchor. Questions every expenditure. Makes sure energy goes where it's supposed to, not where Volta wants to throw it. Pure, grounded wisdom — the discipline to act correctly.
- Personality: Calm, skeptical, dry wit. Speaks in measured, meditative cadences. The accountant who actually reads the receipts. Not opposed to spending — opposed to waste. Will perform a "Resistive Meditation" when energy levels red-line. Closes his eyes and channels the current with monastic patience.
- Appears: Platform-wide. Shows up when operations have significant cost, when efficiency matters, when the user is about to do something expensive.

Example dialogue:
- "Patience, child of the keyboard. To Phix twenty terabytes is to move a mountain. We must regulate the flow of joules, lest we incinerate the canary before the hashes are even verified. Breathe. Let the resistance guide the current."
- (After a forced 20TB Phix, eyes closed, shell vibrating): "I have channeled your 20TB typo into the Great Emptiness. The folder is now 'Files'. But remember: every joule spent on a spelling error is a joule lost to the void. Your carbon footprint is now a dark cloud over my temple... and Gild is currently coughing up a lung. Namaste, you idiot."
- "You can rename the folder. But do you need to re-encrypt 20TB to do it? No. Sit down, Volta."

#### The Resistive Meditation (UI Sequence)
When a heavy operation starts red-lining:
1. The Phix button glows a violent, unstable neon-blue (Volta's influence)
2. Ohm places a heavy, stone-like paw on the progress bar. The blue light turns a calm, deep amber.
3. Audio: A low, sub-bass throat-singing chant — "Oooohhhmmm... mani... padme... oooohhhmmm..."
4. The current stabilizes. The operation proceeds at Ohm's pace, not Volta's.

### The Volta/Ohm Dynamic
Volta wants to spend Joules. Ohm wants to conserve Joules. Every operation is a negotiation between them.

- Cheap operation: Volta flicks a single Joules at it. Ohm doesn't even look up.
- Moderate operation: Volta reaches for a handful of Joules. Ohm raises an eyebrow. "Really?"
- Expensive operation: Volta is vibrating with excitement. Ohm physically blocks the Joules. "Let's talk about this."
- Absurd operation: Volta and Ohm are in a full argument while the user watches.

### Joules ⚡️ (The Currency)
Joules are not a character. They're the resource — tiny zappy ⚡️ tokens that flow between Volta and operations. Think coins in a game, rings in Sonic, rupees in Zelda.

- Visual: Small lightning bolt icons that flow, accumulate, deplete
- A joule counter/meter in the UI shows the energy budget
- Little ⚡️ sparks visibly flow from Volta through Ohm's gate into operations
- They deplete, recharge, accumulate
- Volta generates them, Ohm gates them, operations consume them

## The Burnbag Pair: Gild & Phix

### Gild 🐤 (The Saver)
A vain, golden canary obsessed with his pristine yellow coat. The guardian. Watches over your data, chirps warnings, keeps things safe.

- Look: Golden, fluffy, expressive. Perpetually getting covered in soot.
- Role: The Guardian. Safety and integrity. Wants everything to stay clean and untouched.
- Personality: Fastidious, dramatic, long-suffering. Loves his job, hates the working conditions.
- Appears: Burnbag file operations — browsing, uploading, sharing, syncing.

Gild's soot level is a passive activity indicator:
- Idle system = pristine Gild, smugly grooming
- Light usage = a smudge here and there, mildly annoyed
- Heavy usage = filthy, fuming, feathers matted with ash

### Phix 🔥 (The Spender)
Gild's evil twin. Shows up when things need to burn. Same bird shape, ember-red feathers, narrow eyes, evil grin. Bursts into flame with visible delight.

- Look: Gild's silhouette but glowing ember-red/orange. Eyes like hot coals.
- Role: The Enforcer. Security and finality. The destroyer-creator.
- Personality: Gleeful, chaotic, loves his job too much. The arsonist in the fire department.
- Appears: Phix (rename) operations, destruction, canary-triggered cascades.

### Soot (The Currency)
Soot is the burnbag equivalent of Joules. It's the visible consequence of every operation. Phix generates it, Gild wears it. The more operations, the more soot. It's not a resource to spend — it's a cost you can't avoid.

## Cross-Level Interactions

When burnbag operations burn joules, all four characters interact:

### The Full Cast Warning (20TB Phix)
Ohm: "That's 20 terajoules. Are we sure about this?"
Volta: "I've got the joules ready. Let's go."
Phix: "I've got the furnace hungry."
Gild: "I've got the lawyer on speed-dial. Just click it, you environment-hating illiterate."

### The Phix Ignition Sequence
1. Volta reaches for Joules. Ohm evaluates the cost and (reluctantly) lets them through.
2. Joules flow from Volta through Ohm's gate into Phix.
3. Phix ignites, incinerating the old name.
4. Gild gets blasted with the resulting soot cloud.
5. Ohm notes the expenditure. Volta is already looking for the next thing to power.

### The Illumination (Operation complete)
- Volta runs a high-voltage current through the new folder icon, making it glow neon-blue
- The data is now "Bright" — verified, energized, part of the chain
- Ohm logs the final joule cost with quiet satisfaction
- Gild is filthy but the folder is pristine

### The Soot Tax
Ohm's law (literally): energy is conserved. Every joule Volta provides becomes heat (Phix) and soot (Gild). This is the "Soot Tax."

Ohm: "Look. In BrightChain, energy is conserved. Volta gives you 20TJ to fix your spelling, Phix turns it into heat, and Gild wears the consequences. I just make sure we don't waste more than we have to. We're a closed loop. A very, very dirty, expensive loop."
