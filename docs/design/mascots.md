# BrightChain Mascot & Economic System Master Reference

## Status: Concept / Core Design
## Version: 1.2.0

### 1. The Core Architecture
The BrightChain ecosystem is powered by a two-tiered mascot system. Each tier consists of a "Spender" (Action/Chaos) and a "Saver" (Stability/Order). They are linked by a specific Resource or Consequence.

| Level | Spender (Action) | Saver (Stability) | The Stake |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **BrightChain (Platform)** | **Volta** ⚡️ | **Ohm** 🪨 | **Joules** (Resource/Currency) |
| **Digital Burnbag (Product)** | **Phix** 🔥 | **Gild** 🐤 | **Soot** (Consequence/Karma) |

---

### 2. Platform Layer: The Grid (Volta & Ohm)

#### **Volta ⚡️ (The Spender)**
* **Persona:** The High-Voltage Architect. A living spark—a jagged, neon-blue geometric fox/lynx made of pure electricity.
* **Role:** The Provider. She generates and pushes **Joules** through the system.
* **Personality:** Hyperactive, generous with energy, and impatient with "Resistance."
* **UI Behavior:** She crackles near the Joule-meter. During heavy operations (20TB+), she glows white-hot and vibrates with the desire to execute.

#### **Ohm 🪨 (The Saver)**
* **Persona:** The Stoic Monk of Resistance. A heavy, stone-sloth-turtle with a glowing **Ω** (Ohm) symbol integrated into his shell.
* **Mantra:** *"Ohm mani padme ohm."*
* **Role:** The Gatekeeper/Anchor. He regulates the flow of **Joules** to ensure system integrity.
* **UI Behavior:** During high-load "Phixing," he performs a **Resistive Meditation**. He places a heavy stone paw on the progress bar, turning the energy flow from blue to a calm, resistive amber.

---

### 3. Product Layer: The Burnbag (Gild & Phix)

#### **Gild 🐤 (The Guardian)**
* **Persona:** A vain golden canary. The "Body" of the Burnbag protocol.
* **Role:** The Witness. He guards integrity but wears the **Soot** (consequence) of every operation.
* **The Soot Mechanic:** His feathers darken based on activity. High-Joule operations leave him pitch-black and indignant.
* **Dialogue Style:** Fastidious and long-suffering. *"I just preened! Now I'm a chimney sweep because you can't spell 'Documents'."*

#### **Phix 🔥 (The Enforcer)**
* **Persona:** Gild’s "Fire-Mode" twin. The "Flame" of the Burnbag protocol.
* **Role:** The Destroyer-Creator. He consumes **Joules** to incinerate old data states and rise with the new ones.
* **Personality:** Gleeful and chaotic. He loves the "Phoenix Cycle" (copy-then-destroy).
* **Appearance:** Same silhouette as Gild, but ember-red with eyes like hot coals.

---

### 4. The Stake Elements (Non-Characters)

#### **Joules ⚡️ (The Currency)**
* **Status:** **RESOURCE ONLY.**
* **Visual:** Neon-blue sparks or hexagonal tokens.
* **Flow:** Generated by Volta → Regulated by Ohm → Consumed by Phix. 
* **Friction:** They turn orange/red when passing through Ohm’s "Resistance" during heavy loads.

#### **Soot 💨 (The Consequence)**
* **Status:** **BYPRODUCT ONLY.**
* **Visual:** Dark particles and smoke clouds.
* **Mechanic:** Produced by Phix’s fire; accumulates on Gild’s feathers. It represents the "Carbon Footprint" of the user's digital actions.

---

### 5. Interaction Lifecycle: The 20TB Phix
When a user renames a 20TB directory, the system executes the following "Enlightenment" sequence:

1.  **The Demand:** The UI requests 20,000 Joules for a Full-Cycle operation.
2.  **The Budget:** **Volta** lunges for the **Joules**, eager to power the move.
3.  **The Gate:** **Ohm** initiates a **Resistive Meditation**, regulating the draw so the system stays stable.
4.  **The Heat:** The **Joules** turn orange from friction as they flow through Ohm into the **Burnbag** layer.
5.  **The Burn:** **Phix** catches the energy and erupts, incinerating the old 20TB state.
6.  **The Fallout:** **Gild** is blasted with the resulting **Soot**, turning him pitch-black.
7.  **The Rise:** The operation completes. **Volta** illuminates the new folder in neon-blue. The data is now "Bright."
